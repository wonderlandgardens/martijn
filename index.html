I'm a passionate design director and avid traveller with a deep appreciation for food, travel and gardening.

 

Back in the day, I dreamed of starting an design studio (and a restaurant) in the heart of Amsterdam.

 

Fast forward 9 years and I founded WONDERLAND, a digital brand studio dedicated to creating exceptional brand experiences that leave a lasting impression.

 

With numerous digital awards and successful projects under our belt, we've helped companies achieve digital excellence in the ever-evolving noisy digital landscape.

 

I also founded the HUMAN newsletter - acclaimed by industry peers for curating and writing about various creative topics, with over 38 campaigns to date. I'm also the founder of Sustainable Digital Design , a platform for digital sustainability insights from industry experts and design communities.

 

Over the past two decades I've won many digital awards, including ADCN, FWA, Awwwards, Lovies and a Webby. I judged or spoken at the Webby, Lovies and Awwwards, and before Wonderland I worked at JWT and TBWA.

 

I studied Media & Communication in Amsterdam and then Design in London. (I still have a weakness for interior design though).
