I'm a passionate digital design director with a deep appreciation for food, travel and gardening.

 

Back in the day, I dreamed of starting an design studio (and a restaurant) in the heart of Amsterdam.

 

Fast forward 9 years and I founded WONDERLAND, a digital brand studio dedicated to creating exceptional brand experiences that leave a lasting impression.

 

With numerous digital awards and successful projects under our belt, we've helped companies achieve digital excellence in the ever-evolving noisy digital landscape.

 

I also founded the Creative Currents newsletter - acclaimed by industry peers for curating and writing about various creative topics, with over 40+ campaigns to date. I'm also the founder of Sustainable Digital Design , a platform for digital sustainability insights from industry experts and design communities.

 

Over the past two decades I've won many digital awards, including ADCN, FWA, Awwwards, Webby & Lovies. I judged or spoken at the Webby, Lovies and Awwwards, and before Wonderland I worked at JWT and DDB Tribal.

 

I studied Media & Communication in Amsterdam and then Design in London (I have a hidden weakness for interior design). ).
